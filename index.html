The best feeders are made by hand

Materials

Feeders can be made from wood, plastic bottles, glass jars, old dishes and nets.

Placing

The feeder can be hung both on the side branches of trees, and firmly screwed to the trunk (if there are no cats nearby), a large feeder can be dug into the ground on the trunk. In this case, the pole should be smooth (from cats), and if necessary, contain a protective metal sleeve as a protection against squirrels. Do not place the house on a pole in the middle of a large lawn, as a small hawk may start hunting the birds. In general, make sure that the feeder is inaccessible to predators (cats and martens), for example, do not hang next to a fence, over low thick branches, among low bushes. The most optimal height for the feeder is 1.5 meters.

Menu for birds

The best food for many species is unroasted sunflower seeds. If you make a grain mixture for feeding yourself, the amount of sunflower can be up to 50% (no more). Other ingredients are millet, oatmeal, grass seeds. Unsalted lard on a string is respected by blackbirds, titmice and wagtails. Slightly soaked dry apples and raisins, as well as fresh apples, pumpkin, carrots, and corn are also eaten by blackbirds. For jays, woodpeckers and woodpeckers, you can prepare unroasted unsalted peanuts, melon seeds, pumpkin, and pine cones. Blackbirds and guests from the far north - mistletoe - will enjoy the berries of rowan, rosehip, hawthorn and others.
